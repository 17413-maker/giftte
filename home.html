About Gifté+

Gifté+ is more than just a gifting app — it’s your smart companion for finding meaningful presents that actually connect with people. Built on principles of personalization, psychology, and ethical intelligence, Gifté+ transforms how people discover and give gifts.

Our vision is to make gifting effortless, emotional, and ethical. Whether you’re picking something for a friend, a colleague, or a loved one, Gifté+ helps you choose the right gift that tells the right story.

We believe every gift should carry emotion, purpose, and thoughtfulness. That’s why our AI learns from personality, interests, and preferences — not random data scraping — to make your gifts feel truly personal.

Our Core Values

Ethical AI

Our systems are trained with a focus on fairness and privacy. We never exploit or misuse data — we use it responsibly to serve users better.

Privacy First

Your data belongs to you. Gifté+ never stores or sells your information. All insights are processed securely and used only to enhance your gift suggestions.

Smart Personalization

We blend AI logic with emotional understanding. Each recommendation is crafted to match personality types, occasions, and sentiment.

Ethical OSINT

Our OSINT integrations are built with ethical boundaries. We gather open, consented, and publicly available data only to make predictions — never to invade privacy.

Human-Centered Design

From color to layout, every element of Gifté+ is built to create warmth and simplicity — making the digital feel personal again.

Future Ready

We’re building the future of gifting with AI. Gifté+ learns, evolves, and adapts — ensuring your experience gets better with every search.
